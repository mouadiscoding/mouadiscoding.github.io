Speech Enhancement Using Transformers and U-Net

This project utilizes a hybrid architecture combining U-Net and Transformer models for speech enhancement. The model is trained to remove various types of noise from audio signals, enhancing the clarity and quality of speech. We have tested the model on several noise conditions, demonstrating its effectiveness across different environments.
